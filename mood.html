Premium Mood Tracker

How does the Mood Tracker work?

1. Enter your name in the provided input field.

2. Click on the happy or sad emoji to select your mood.

3. Click the Submit button to record your mood.

4. The counters will keep track of the number of happy and sad moods.

5. The total mood score will be calculated based on the selected moods.

6. The percent bar will show the distribution of happy moods.

7. The message below will provide feedback based on the mood data.

8. Use the Refresh button to reset the mood tracker.

9. The Export Data button allows you to download the recorded mood data.
